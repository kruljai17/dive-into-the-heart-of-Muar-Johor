ROAD NETWORK

Muar is served by a well-maintained road network, making it accessible by car from major cities and towns in Malaysia. The North-South Expressway (E2) is a major highway connecting Muar to Kuala Lumpur north and Johor Bahru south. Federal Route 5 is another important route that runs through the town, connecting Muar to nearby areas like Batu Pahat and Melaka.

PUBLIC BUSES

The public bus system in Muar is a convenient and affordable mode of transportation for locals and visitors. Muar's main bus terminal, Bentayan Bus Terminal, is centrally located and serves as a hub for intercity and local buses. These buses connect Muar to other parts of Johor and neighboring states. Local bus services provide connectivity within the town, making commuting to different neighborhoods and attractions easy.

TAXIS AND RIDE-HAILING SERVICES

Taxis are readily available in Muar, offering a flexible transportation option for those who prefer a more direct and private mode of travel. Additionally, ride-hailing services like Grab operate in Muar, providing a convenient and efficient way to get around the town and its surroundings.
