WETEX PARADE SHOPPING CENTRE

Wetex Parade Shopping Centre, conveniently located in the city centre, offers a variety of local shops, larger chains, and eateries. From clothing and jewellery to books and electronics, you can find good deals and enjoy a range of dining options, including Western franchises and local favourites.

MUAR CLOCK TOWER

Located in the middle of a roundabout, the Muar Clock Tower is a striking three-tiered structure painted in white and blue. While it’s closed to the public, its colonial aesthetic makes it a notable landmark to admire from a distance. The clock still functions, and the tower lights up beautifully at night.

MAHARANI MURAL LANE

Muar's vibrant street art scene is best experienced at Maharani Mural Lane. This laneway is filled with murals ranging from Disney-inspired artwork to historical scenes depicting life in Muar. The eclectic mix of art offers a fun and engaging way to explore the city’s creative side.
