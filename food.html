MEE BANDUNG ABU HANIPAH

Originating from Muar, Mee Bandung is a must-try local delight for its unique and mildly spicy taste. Several food stalls serve this special dish, but most people believe that the cook at Abu Bakar Hanipah has mastered the art of Mee Bandung. Their Mee Bandung has a rich and thick prawn flavor. The combination of their specially made sambal, shrimps, cuttlefish, and beef results in this savory and slightly overpowering taste that will linger long in your taste buds.

Location: 69-A, Jalan Abdullah, Muar, Johor, Malaysia

Opening Hours: Daily 7:00 am - 4:00 pm

MUAR SOUP HOUSE

Occupying three shophouses in Muar, it’s hard to imagine that Muar Soup House was once only a small pedicab stall selling various soups at the roadside. However, what’s not changing is that beef and mutton soup are still their major draw. The humble bowl of soup is overloaded with mutton or beef according to what you order, and you wouldn’t find any gamey taste of raw meat. The soup will exceed your expectations and be robust, thick, and give off a slightly herbal smell.

Location: 47, Jalan Sisi, Muar, Johor, Malaysia. (Near Classic Hotel)

Opening Hours: Daily 24 Hours

MUAR BAKRI LAKSA

Knowing that Muar Bakri Laksa is renowned as one of the Top 50 Best Laksa Outlets in Malaysia, as a food enthusiast, you should not miss it if you happen to be around Muar. Seethed for 3 hours, the broth is a complex of many spices and flavourful ingredients, bringing enjoyment to customers' taste buds. The bowl of laksa even contains a generous amount of side ingredients. You can also request to maximise the spiciness – if you can handle it.

Location: 1, 307-4 Batu, 3/4, Jalan Bakri, 84000 Muar, Johor, Malaysia. ( Beside Bakri Petrol Shell Station / Opposite Taman Perdana )

Opening Hours: Daily 6:00 pm - 11:00 pm (Closed on Tuesday)

SAI KEE COFFEE 434

Producing its own brand, Elephant brand coffee, Sai Kee Coffee 434 has served only its own coffee since its establishment. If you like its coffee, ground beans packed in bags can be bought at the shop and enjoyed at home. The coffee at Sai Kee is traditionally made by pouring boiled water over ground beans in muslin. Fragrant, robust, and bitter are the simplest descriptions of this marvelous coffee.

Location: 121, Jalan Maharani, Taman Sri Emas, 84000 Muar, Johor, Malaysia. ( Across The Road From The Central Bus Station And Wet Market )

Opening Hours: Daily 8:00 am - 5:30 pm

SABOK AWOR SEAFOOD COURT

Otherwise known as Medan Ikan Bakar Sabok Awor, this place offers a unique and satisfying seafood dining experience. With a food court concept, the restaurant provides ample and comfortable seating space. Another attraction is that there are about 12 lots that offer a variety of menus you can try. Grilled fish is at the top of the list at this place. However, other special dishes exist, such as spicy and sour fish and ikan tiga rasa. There are grilled or boiled scallops, sambal mussels, deep-fried squid, buttermilk prawns and more. This seafood food court is indeed the best.

Location: Medan Selera, Jalan Cahaya, Sabak Awor, 84000 Muar, Johor, Malaysia

Opening Hours: Daily 5:00 am – 11:59 pm
