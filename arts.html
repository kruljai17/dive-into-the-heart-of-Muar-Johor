MUAR LEATHER CRAFT

Discover the artistry of leather craftsmanship by visiting Muar Leather Craft. Here, you can see the meticulous process of creating high-quality leather products and even purchase handmade leather goods as souvenirs.

MUSIC

Traditional music, including gamelan and kompang ensembles, plays a significant role in Muar’s cultural identity. These traditional forms are often performed at weddings, religious ceremonies, and cultural festivals. Additionally, modern musical genres have gained popularity, with local bands and artists contributing to the town’s dynamic music scene.

MUAR POTTERY

Muar has a long history of pottery-making, with local artisans producing beautiful ceramics and clay products. The designs often incorporate traditional motifs and are highly valued for their craftsmanship.
