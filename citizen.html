TRADITIONAL MALAY VILLAGES

Kampung Sungai Raya and Kampung Parit Jawa

Immerse yourself in the traditional Malay way of life by visiting Kampung Sungai Raya and Kampung Parit Jawa. In Kampung Sungai Raya, witness the intricate art of Malay woodcarving, a skill passed down through generations. Meanwhile, Kampung Parit Jawa offers a glimpse into the world of traditional Malay pottery. Explore the villages' wooden houses, engage with the friendly locals, and appreciate the traditional handicrafts that define these communities.

TRADITIONAL PERFORMANCES

Local Cultural Centre

Attend traditional cultural performances at the local cultural center to experience the beauty and grace of Malay arts. Enjoy mesmerizing traditional dances like Joget and be enchanted by the soothing sounds of Gamelan music. These performances provide a deep dive into the artistic traditions of the Malay community.

MULTICULTURAL HARMONY

Muar is a melting pot of various ethnicities, including Malays, Chinese, Indians, and others. This diversity is reflected in the town’s festivals, traditions, and daily life. Major festivals such as Hari Raya Aidilfitri, Chinese New Year, Deepavali, and Wesak Day are celebrated enthusiastically, showcasing the town’s harmonious coexistence.
