A GLIMPSE INTO THE HISTORY OF MUAR

Muar, a district rich in history and cultural diversity, is located northwest of Johor state, Malaysia. Officially declared the Muar Municipal Council by the Chief Minister of Johor on April 14, 2001, Muar's historical significance stretches back centuries.

From its early beginnings and colonial conflicts to its brief period of sovereignty, Muar's history showcases its enduring significance in Malaysia's historical and cultural landscape.
